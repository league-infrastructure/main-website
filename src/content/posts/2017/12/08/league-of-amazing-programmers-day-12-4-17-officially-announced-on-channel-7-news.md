<p>Channel 7 news announces that San Diego City Council is declaring December 4, 2017 officially “The League of Amazing Programmers Day”. The League is recognized for its after-school program dedicated to teaching professional-level computer programming to a wide range of young San Diegans and for the achievement of teams competing in winning the 2017 D4SD challenge.  <a href="https://youtu.be/1qG6hMfeGNc">Watch this great story!</a></p>
